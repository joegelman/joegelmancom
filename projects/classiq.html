Classiq

web copy, brand voice
visit site ↗

The Project

Explain what's never been done before. Speak to genius visionaries about an experimental quantum computing project. Oh, and do it all in a way that's both conversational and inspirational. Cool.

The Strategy

Shape the Future of Quantum Software. Classiq created an abstraction layer to convert basic instructions into gate-level code. This unlocks brand new possibilities in quantum software.

The Results

Quantum leaps and bounds. Classiq hadn't even released a finished project, but right after rebrand, they raised a $10.5M series A. The strength of the new messaging helped them get investors and the world excited about the quantum possibilities yet to come.
