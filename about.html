In 2018, I left New York for Tel Aviv. Stopped freelancing as a content marketer, and started on the path to hi-tech copywriting.

After writing for Israel's premier cybersecurity VC, YL Ventures, and top technology PR firm, Headline Media, I finally landed at a magical branding workshop called Atreo.

Atreo is the world leader in B2B technology branding. I first learned the ropes as Branding Copywriter from some of the best in the business. Then, in early 2021, I was passed the torch as the Head of Brand Messaging. It's been a wild ride, and the best is yet to come.
