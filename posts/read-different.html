Read different.

If you want to get great at what you're writing now, read great writing that's way different

Always shop for quality, but let your peripheral vision sweep the proverbial bookshelf. Don't skip even the obscurest genres.

In fact, the more "out of your wheelhouse" it feels, the more helpful it may be.

That's not to say stop reading the kind of things you're writing. But you'll likely keep doing that without really trying.

But the truly excellent article or novella you never thought to read, that takes conscious hunting. And that'll kick up the type of lateral, left-field thinking that could be your next breakthrough or godsend of inspiration.

I'm sharing some non-copy, non-b2b stuff I've been reading recently down in the comments. If you've got any recommendations, I'd love if you shared those too.
