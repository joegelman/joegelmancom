Are you interesting?

Trying to be interesting makes you tedious. Try being interested instead, and you’ll become a breath of fresh air. You’ll truly listen, instead of waiting your turn to speak. You’ll capture people’s attention and win their trust. You’ll learn a ton, and teach people in return.

Make a conscious effort to be interested in the world around you, and just like that, you’ll become infinitely more interesting.
