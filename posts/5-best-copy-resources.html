The 5 most badass copywriting resources on the web

  1. Marketing Examples - incredible collection of copy examples, annotated with short, high-value analyses of what works

  2. Very Good Copy -Eddie Shleyner runs this epic newsletter that’s consistently packing the best copywriting tips and inspiration out there

  3. DGMG -Dave Gerhardt is not only one of the freshest voices in B2B marketing, he also hosts a damn good swipefile showcasing a curated list of advertising gems

  4. The Reeder - Gong’s legendary content mastermind, Devin Reed has a killer newsletter brimming with punchy writing/marketing insights

  5. Really Good Emails - like the name suggests, the focus here is email — but there’s plenty of general-purpose copy wisdom to glean from here, too
