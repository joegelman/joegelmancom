New year, new journey

As 2021 ended, so did my time at Atreo.

Goodbyes are never easy. But in this case, it was particularly hard.

In just two short years, I learnt so much and gained what feels like a lifetime of growth and experiences.

From the incredible brands whose stories I helped write. To my wonderful team. To the transformational mentors and role-models I had the privilege of working alongside and learning immeasurably from.

Words can't express the gratitude and appreciation I have looking back on my time at Atreo.

At the same time, I'm also looking forward to the tremendous opportunity up ahead.

As of today, I begin a new journey as the Brand Director for a startup on a truly astounding growth trajectory.

They're still in stealth, so there's not much more detail I can go into.

But expect to hear a lot more, very soon.

Onwards and upwards for 2022!
