Today’s above-and-beyond is tomorrow’s table stakes

Used to be a company Christmas party was all the company “culture” you needed

Now if you don’t have a whole “employee wellness” department you’re basically a hollering band of savages

Used to be design was pretty decoration you sprinkled on top of whatever else it was you were doing

Now design thinking is demanded at the highest level, all the way down to the smallest details — and yes, people are paying attention, and they can tell the difference

Used to be all you had to do was be a bit better/cheaper/smarter than the competition

Now you’ve got to position yourself in a game-changing way, otherwise you’ll drown in a sea of sameness

When you’re setting up your targets, make sure you’re not just catching up with what used to be the standard, and actually aiming beyond what will be mere table stakes tomorrow
