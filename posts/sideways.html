Sideways

Sometimes, when things go sideways it gives you fresh perspective.

Once you get over the shock, you realize things look differently from this new point of view.

And when you collect yourself, and get back up again, you see that maybe sideways was actually the best way forward -- the path you'd been aiming for all along.
