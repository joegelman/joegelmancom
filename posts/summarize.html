Make any piece of writing better in 3 simple steps

  1. Take your writing, and summarize it as much as you can. Then wait a few hours.

  2. Take your summary, and summarize it as much as you can. Then wait a few hours.

  3. Take your new summary, and summarize it as much as you can. Then you're done.

You'll be shocked how well this works.
